Chicken Adobo Recipe

A classic Filipino dish with fried chicken goodness!

Ingredients

  • cooking oil
  • 1 kg of chicken cut ups (preferrably thigh part)
  • 2 tbsp vinegar
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1 cup water
  • 3-5 pieces of bay leaves
  • 1 tsp of whole black peppercorns
  • 2 pieces of Knorr chicken cubes
  • brown sugar

Instructions

  1. Rinse chicken with water and salt
  2. Pour vinegar, soy sauce, and bay leaves into the pot. Make sure NOT to mix.
  3. Slowly put the chicken into the pot and allow it to boil for atleast 20 minutes until vinegar scent fades
  4. Once done, allow chicken to to rest, then fry on a separate pan after a few minutes
  5. Bring back chicken into the vinegar-soy sauce mixture, then add sugar and chilis. Adjust to your liking.
